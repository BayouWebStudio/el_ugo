The Artist

Ugo

Rooted in the centuries-old tradition of Japanese tattooing, Ugo brings mythological compositions to life at Guru Tattoo in San Diego. From Hannya masks that guard and warn to peonies that bloom with quiet power, every piece is a dialogue between skin and story.

His work has graced convention floors from Mondial du Tatouage in Paris to Winnipeg, earning recognition for compositions that honor tradition while commanding contemporary presence.
