How It Works

Submit your consultation request through the form. I'll review your idea and respond within a few days to discuss design direction, timeline, and scheduling.

Deposit

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. The deposit amount varies based on the scale of the project and will be discussed during our consultation. It is applied toward your final session cost.

What to Expect

Large-scale Japanese work is a collaborative process. We'll discuss reference material, composition, and flow before any needles touch skin. Custom designs are drawn specifically for your body and vision — no flash, no repeats.

Cancellation Policy

Please provide at least 48 hours notice for any changes. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in forfeiture of your deposit.
